## ğŸ”§ Troubleshooting Guide

### Issue 1: Instagram Upload Fails

**Symptoms**: Upload stuck at 0%, error message appears

**Solutions**:
1. **Check internet**: Ensure stable Wi-Fi or cellular connection
2. **Restart app**: Close Instagram, reopen
3. **Check file size**: Ensure video is <100MB
4. **Check duration**: Ensure 15-30 seconds (Instagram Reels limit: 90s)
5. **Check format**: Ensure MP4 format
6. **Try later**: Instagram servers may be busy

### Issue 2: Hashtags Not Clickable

**Symptoms**: Hashtags appear as plain text, not blue links

**Solutions**:
1. **Remove spaces**: Ensure no spaces in hashtags (e.g., `#KGM` not `# KGM`)
2. **Check language**: Korean hashtags may not always be clickable
3. **Verify spelling**: Ensure exact spelling: `#ë¬´ì˜ë§¨ì–´ì›Œì¦ˆ`
4. **Wait**: Hashtags may take 1-2 minutes to become clickable

### Issue 3: Video Quality Degraded on Instagram

**Symptoms**: Video looks blurry or pixelated after upload

**Solutions**:
1. **Upload from Wi-Fi**: Cellular may compress more
2. **Check source quality**: Ensure original is 1080x1920
3. **Wait for processing**: Instagram may take 5-10 minutes to process HD
4. **Re-upload**: Delete and upload again
5. **Instagram limitation**: Some compression is normal

### Issue 4: Cannot Copy Instagram URL

**Symptoms**: "Copy Link" option not available

**Solutions**:
1. **Make public**: Ensure Reel is public (not private)
2. **Wait for processing**: URL may not be available immediately
3. **Manual copy**: Open Reel in browser, copy URL from address bar
4. **Share via DM**: Share to yourself, copy link from DM

### Issue 5: Google Form Won't Accept Video File

**Symptoms**: File upload fails, "File too large" error

**Solutions**:
1. **Check file size**: Must be <100MB (verify with `ls -lh`)
2. **Compress video**: Use HandBrake or online compressor
3. **Upload to Drive**: Upload to Google Drive, share link in form
4. **Contact organizer**: Email contest organizer for alternative submission

### Issue 6: Missed Deadline

**Symptoms**: Submitted after Feb 19, 2026

**Solutions**:
1. **Check timezone**: Deadline may be in Korean time (KST)
2. **Contact organizer**: Explain situation, request exception
3. **Screenshot proof**: Show work-in-progress timestamps
4. **Next contest**: Look for future KGM contests
